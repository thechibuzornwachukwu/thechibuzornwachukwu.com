ABOUT ME

Chibuzor Joshua Nwachukwu is an unrepentant reformer poised to remove the claims of self-imposed limitation. That is, the plague evident in the thought process of many-a-persons, making them everything as a tangible excuse to NOT create a better future.

In 2021, he acted in aligment with predestination, making a life-changing decision. Although qualified to study at a reonowned university in Nigeria, Chibuzor took a big risk, one that, in hindsight, was clearly the hand of God. He pivoted from Pharmacy, with one step back, and a giant leap into the world of technology with computer science. For him, it paid off.

Ever since, he has become a multipotentialite, with extensive experience as a data analyst, brand designer, AI engineer, & product manager. As for problem solving, he tackles them by combining technical depth with entrepreneurship & strategy, bridging AI, analytics, and identity engineering.

Today, Chibuzor Nwachukwu is very passionate about problems that are overlooked in his clime. Therefore, where others optimize for vanity metrics, he drives action for meaningful outcomes.
